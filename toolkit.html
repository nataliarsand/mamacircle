Toolkit

This page includes simple ways you can help others discover the Circle. Whether you’ve been helped or want to offer support, here’s how you can spread the word, softly and kindly.

Share the circle. Let it ripple out. 💞

Say it in your words

Share one of these little messages in your bio, stories, group chats, or anywhere it feels right:

  • I’m part of #mamacircle 💞, you can always reach out to me (if it feels safe for you).

  • You’re not alone. Look up #mamacircle 💞, a gentle way to find other mamas.

  • #mamacircle 💞 is not a group or app, just a quiet signal of care between mamas.

  • I'm part of #mamacircle 💞, gentle support from mama to mama.

Want to invite someone?

Here are two gentle messages you can copy, paste, and adapt:

For a new mama

Hey mama, just wanted to say, I’m here if you ever need to talk, cry, vent, or just not feel so alone. I’m part of #mamacircle 💞, a gentle way mamas show up for each other. If you're curious, there's more about it here: mamacircle.me

For a mama who wants to support others

You have such a kind heart, I think you'd love this grassroots project called #mamacircle 💞. It's a way for mamas to gently say “I'm here if you need me.” You can simply add the hashtag to your profile and read more here: mamacircle.me

Add the signal to your bio

Let other mamas know you’re part of the circle:

#mamacircle 💞

That’s it. That’s the magic.
