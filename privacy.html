Privacy & Safety

Last updated: March 2025

What is Mama Circle?

Mama Circle is a grassroots, community-led initiative. We exist to connect mothers who wish to support each other with honesty, softness, and care. This is not a company, coaching service, or moderated forum. You are joining a self-organized collective of mamas supporting mamas.

What personal data do we collect?

When you fill out the Mama Circle Supporter Form, we collect and display only the information you choose to share — like your first name, city, Instagram handle, and message.

We do not track website visitors, use cookies, or collect any additional data automatically.

How is your data used?

Your submitted information is published on the mamacircle.me website so new mothers can find someone to talk to. You are publicly declaring yourself part of #mamacircle. You can remove or edit your info at any time — just email us.

Community & Safety Disclaimer

Mama Circle does not moderate interactions, verify identities, or organize meetups. By appearing on this website, you agree to connect with others at your own discretion and risk.

We ask that everyone approach this space with kindness, respect, and boundaries — and understand that we cannot be held responsible for individual behavior.

GDPR & Global Data Rights

We respect your right to privacy. Under the GDPR and other global data laws, you have the right to access, change, or delete your personal information at any time. To make a request, email: hello@mamacircle.me.
