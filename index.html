Mama Circle

#mamacircle 💞 — a soft place to land in motherhood

If you're a new mama feeling overwhelmed, emotional, or simply in need of a kind word — this space is for you. You are not alone. This circle exists to remind you that support is real and close, even when it doesn’t feel that way.

You might come across someone who says:
“I’m part of #mamacircle — you can always reach out to me.”
And they’ll mean it.

The Mama Circle Manifesto

When you're tired — we offer understanding.
When you're overwhelmed — we offer calm.
When you're unsure — we offer stories.
When you're lonely — we offer connection.
When you're lost — we offer each other.
And if we’re nearby — we’ll bring tea, hold your baby, clean your kitchen, or simply give you a hug.
  • Connection over isolation
  • Listening over advising
  • Presence over perfection
  • Compassion over comparison
  • Community over competition

This is the spirit of Mama Circle — not about being perfect, but being present.

How Mama Circle Works

You're not alone.

If you're a new mother feeling overwhelmed, emotional, or just needing a kind word, this is for you.

You might be here because someone shared the link with you, or you saw #mamacircle 💞 in their bio or message. That person is letting you know: you can reach out to them. You can also:

  • Scroll through the Circle and read the messages.
  • Reach out to anyone who feels like a soft landing.
  • Send a message, voice note, or DM — just say hi.
  • No sign-up required. No expectations. Just connection.

Show your support.

If you're already a mama and want to be there for others, you can:

  • Share the Circle Signal when you meet another mother:
    I’m part of #mamacircle 💞 — you can always reach out to me.
  • Add #mamacircle 💞 to your Instagram or WhatsApp bio so others know you're part of the Circle.
  • Optionally, fill out the form to be listed publicly so new mamas can find and message you.
  • You can be part of the Circle with or without being listed.

Mama Circle isn’t a group or an app — just a quiet way for mothers to find each other, one message at a time.
