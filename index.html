Mama Circle — a soft place to land in motherhood

If you're a new mama feeling overwhelmed, emotional, or simply in need of a kind word — this space is for you.

Mama Circle is not a group or an app. It's a quiet signal — a simple way for mamas to find one another through #mamacircle 💞.

You might come across someone who says:
I’m part of #mamacircle 💞 — you can always reach out to me (if it feels safe for you).
And they’ll mean it.

The Mama Circle Manifesto

  • When you're tired — we offer understanding.
  • When you're overwhelmed — we offer calm.
  • When you're unsure — we offer stories.
  • When you're lonely — we offer connection.
  • When you're lost — we offer each other.
  • And if we’re nearby — we’ll bring tea, hold your baby, clean your kitchen, or simply give you a hug.
  • Connection over isolation
  • Listening over advising
  • Presence over perfection
  • Compassion over comparison
  • Community over competition

These are the values of Mama Circle — not about being perfect, but being present.

How Mama Circle Works

You're not alone.

You might be here because someone shared this link, or you saw #mamacircle 💞 in someone’s bio or message. That person is letting you know:

You can always reach out to me (if it feels safe for you).

You can also:

  • Search #mamacircle 💞 on your social media.
  • Reach out to someone who feels like a soft landing.
  • Send a message, ask a question, or just say hi.
  • No sign-up required. No expectations. Just connection.

Show your support.

If you’re already a mama and want to gently hold space for others, you can:

  • Say the Circle Signal when you meet another mother: I’m part of #mamacircle 💞 — you can always reach out to me (if it feels safe for you).
  • Add #mamacircle 💞 to your social media bio, so others know you’re part of the circle.
  • Visit the Toolkit for messages, ideas, and gentle ways to spread the word.
  • You don’t need to be perfect, just present. No sign-ups, no lists — just love in motion.

There’s no app. No group. Just a circle held together by quiet connection — one message, one mama at a time.
